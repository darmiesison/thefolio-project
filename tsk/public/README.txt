Portfolio Topic: All About Cats

1. Topic Choice
I chose cats as my portfolio topic because they are one of the most popular and beloved pets worldwide. I wanted to highlight their unique behaviors, history, care tips, and the joy they bring to human lives. This topic allowed me to combine informative content with visually engaging images to make the website both educational and appealing to visitors.

2. Key Design Decisions

Color Scheme: I used a soft pastel palette with lilac and pink tones to give the site a warm, friendly, and approachable feel.

Typography: Two fonts were used for clarity and style: Poppins for headings and Open Sans for body text.

Layout: Each page has a consistent header and footer for easy navigation. The homepage includes a hero section with an image slider and preview sections for the other pages. Content sections are visually separated using cards, lists, and boxes with subtle shadows for emphasis.

Responsiveness: The layout is responsive with media queries for mobile and desktop screens, ensuring a smooth experience on different devices.

Interactive Elements: Hover effects on links and buttons enhance user interaction. A continuous image slider on the homepage adds dynamic visual interest.

3. Forms Structure

Contact Form (contact.html): Includes Name, Email (placeholder-only), and Message fields, with a Submit button. This form does not have backend functionality, serving as a front-end demonstration of how visitors can reach out.

Registration Form (register.html): Includes Full Name, Preferred Username, Date of Birth (HTML5 date input), radio buttons for interest level, a checkbox for agreeing to terms, and a Submit button. The form fields are properly labeled and aligned with CSS for clarity. All personal info uses placeholder text to comply with submission rules.
